About Us! Hello! Welcome to our CSC 642 class project! We are group 1 and our team consists of 6 computer science college students: Rita Belu Velazco, Justin Su, Tom Kirke, Benjamin Dinh, Jooho Chang, and William Ambriz. Our website, Lil' Chef, is a website where users can find community within cooking! This is the social media for all rookie, beginner, or master chefs out there to share all sorts of recipes with the world! Our goal is to inspire people to find joy and passion in cooking. We worked hard to create this idea of Lil' Chef, so we hope you enjoy!
